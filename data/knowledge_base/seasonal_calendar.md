# MerchMix Seasonal Calendar — 2026

## Overview

This calendar defines retail seasons, key events, and their impact on demand patterns. Used for seasonal adjustments in demand forecasting and inventory pre-builds.

## Annual Season Definitions

| Season | Start | End | Key Categories Affected |
|--------|-------|-----|------------------------|
| Spring | Mar 1 | May 31 | Lightweight apparel, sneakers, accessories |
| Summer | Jun 1 | Aug 31 | T-shirts, shorts, caps, outdoor items |
| Fall Transition | Sep 1 | Oct 15 | Hoodies, boots, layers, long sleeves |
| Holiday | Oct 16 | Dec 31 | All categories (gifting peak) |
| Winter Clearance | Jan 1 | Feb 28 | Outerwear markdowns, basics replenishment |

## Seasonal Transition Rules for Inventory

### Pre-Season Build (8 weeks before season start)
- Begin increasing inventory for upcoming season's key categories
- Target: +1.0 MIO above normal target for seasonal items
- Priority: Fast-moving (Class A) items first

### In-Season Management
- Monitor sell-through rate weekly
- If sell-through < 60% at mid-season, begin markdown planning
- If sell-through > 80% at mid-season, trigger emergency reorder

### End-of-Season Wind-Down (4 weeks before season end)
- Reduce reorder quantities by 50%
- Flag items with MIO > 2.0 for markdown
- Do NOT reorder seasonal items unless sell-through > 90%

### Carryover Policy
- Core basics (year-round): Carry over normally
- Seasonal fashion: Max 15% of peak inventory carried to next season
- Accessories: Carryover OK for classics, markdown fashion accessories
